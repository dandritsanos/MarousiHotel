Since 1998, Hotel Maroussi has been a legendary city hotel in the northern suburbs of Athens. Just steps from OAKA Olympic Stadium, major business and hospital hubs, and the city’s premier shopping centers, it offers trusted hospitality with effortless access to the airport and city center.
