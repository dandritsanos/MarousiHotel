Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 May 2025

At Maroussi Hotel, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR").

2. Why We Collect It

We collect your data to manage your booking requests, improve our services, and communicate with you about your stay.

3. Legal Basis

Your data is processed based on your explicit consent when submitting a booking request.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your data is securely stored and accessible only by authorized staff.
