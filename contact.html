Location

Hotel Maroussi is set in the heart of the northern suburbs, close to Athens’ main business and medical hubs, OAKA Olympic Stadium, and the city’s largest shopping centers. With excellent transport links, both the airport and city center are within easy reach.

  • 300 meters from Neratziotissa metro & suburban railway (direct connection to the airport and city center)
  • 5 minutes from The Mall Athens and Golden Hall, two of the city’s premier shopping destinations
  • Moments from OAKA Olympic Stadium, hosting major sports and cultural events
  • Quick access to Maroussi’s business district and leading hospital hubs

Since 1998, Hotel Maroussi has been part of the neighborhood’s story, a long-standing landmark offering guests a trusted base where convenience meets comfort.
