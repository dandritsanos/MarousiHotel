About Hotel Maroussi

A trusted stay since 1998

Since 1998, Hotel Maroussi has been a steady presence in the northern suburbs of Athens, one of the few hotels in the area to have remained in continuous operation. Over the years, it has quietly welcomed business travelers, visitors to nearby hospitals, and guests attending events at OAKA, just moments away. Its location combines convenience and calm, within walking distance of the metro, The Mall Athens, and Golden Hall, with direct train connections to both the airport and the city center in only 25 minutes. With its long-standing heritage and trusted hospitality, Hotel Maroussi offers comfort and ease and is a landmark hotel in the area.
