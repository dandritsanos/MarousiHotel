About Hotel Maroussi

Hotel Maroussi is a welcoming retreat in the Marousi district of Athens, offering bright and stylish rooms — some featuring spa baths for added comfort. Conveniently located just 300 meters from Neratziotissa metro and train station and a short walk from The Mall shopping center, it provides easy access to both business hubs and leisure destinations. Athens landmarks such as the historic Plaka district, Omonia Square, and the Acropolis Museum are all within approximately 5.5 km, while national road access and public transport connections make exploring the city seamless. Guests enjoy modern amenities, free Wi-Fi, 24-hour room service, and the charm of elegantly decorated rooms with wrought-iron or upholstered beds.

National road access is seamless, and public transportation to the aforementioned destinations is readily available.
